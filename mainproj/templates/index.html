About the Site

Nowadays, in the era of Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn, there is no shortage of personal profile sites that can be found online. At times, it seems as though an unending pestilence of them are mercilessly being spewed across the World Wide Web. In a poetic sort of irony, each profile is built by someone who, in a futile attempt to separate him/herself from the rest of the pack, ends up creating the same brand of content as everyone else. A grim state of affairs indeed...

This website, of course, will be no exception!!! It is my own attempt to cram one more self-congratulatory bio page into the Internet's already bloated warehouse of contrived personal profiles.

So feel free to page through my short slideshow below. It gives a (very) brief overview of my professional work experience, as well as a more in depth look at a few hobbies/projects I have been working on outside of my full-time job. Also, you can check out my aviation/flight planning website live here (or by clicking on the tiny airplane logo in the navbar), which is another small side project I have put together. Lastly, go to the Documentation section of this site for my Resume and Certifications.
